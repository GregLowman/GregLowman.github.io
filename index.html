About Me

I'm a Computer Science and Physics double major with a minor in Quantum Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder, transferred from Florida State College at Jacksonville where I completed my Associate's degree.

My focus is quantum computing — specifically research and development. I believe quantum systems have the potential to power high-fidelity simulations that could meaningfully accelerate both lab work and scientific research. My goal is to be part of a team making that happen.

My most recent project was a semester-long perovskite solar cell degradation study — working with teammates, I led all the programming work: data collection pipelines, statistical analysis, visualization, and interpretation of results in Python.

I'm actively developing my research skills and applying my CS background to real scientific problems, while also shipping full-stack products and automation tools along the way.
